# June 2026

Welcome to the June 2026 OpenScienceLab Update!

### Changes:

We've migrated our conda environments to [Pixi](https://pixi.prefix.dev/latest/workspace/environment/)

Pixi offers improved speed and customizability. Read this [guide](https://pixi.prefix.dev/latest/switching_from/conda/) to learn more about how and why to switch from using conda to Pixi.

Our conda environments are still available to use, but it is recommended to use the replacement Pixi environments.
